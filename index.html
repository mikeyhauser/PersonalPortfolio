Mechanical: I Can Fix That Engine!

For over a decade I have repaired engines, both diesel and gasoline, in an array of industries. From agricultural and trucking equipment in Orange County, NC, to buses and tourism boats in the Galapagos Islands, the world of mechanical repair has been kind in providing me with a myriad of opportunity to disassemble equipment and fix it. Whether in the field or in a workshop, every mechanical breakdown is a valuable chance to plumb the depths of critical thinking. I owe every shattered bearing my gratitude for connecting me in the web of society.
