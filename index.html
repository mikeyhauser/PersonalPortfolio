MECHANICAL REPAIR

For over a decade I have repaired engines, both diesel and gasoline, in an array of industries. It brings me joy to disassemble equipment and fix it. Whether in the field or in a workshop, every mechanical breakdown is a valuable chance to plumb the depths of critical thinking. I owe every shattered bearing my gratitude.
